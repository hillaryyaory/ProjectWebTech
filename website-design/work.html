Luke's Lobster
Liberty Society worked with Luke's Lobster this Christmas. I was in charge of designing stockings. I decided to add lobsters, prawns, and crabs to the stocking as it represented their seafood selection.

We had multiple designs for the stockings. However, after much consultation, Luke Lobster told us that The blue color was the same color as their logo.

Luke Lobster loved our design and bought over 150 stockings from us! By Christmas, all the Luke Lobster branches in Singapore hanged our stockings all over our their store!
